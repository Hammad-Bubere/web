Tips for creating Strong password manully

  • Use alphabets in both upper and lower cases.
  • Use numbers.
  • Use special charachters like (!,@,#,$,%,^,*,etc).
  • Password should be at least 8 charachters.
  • Don't use one password every where.
  • Change your password at least once in a year.
